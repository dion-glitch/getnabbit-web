Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save from WhatsApp or iMessage?

Select the text you want to save, tap the Share button, and choose Nabbit from the share sheet. Nabbit will automatically detect the type of information (address, phone number, date, etc.) and save it for you.

How do I search with Siri?

Say "Hey Siri, search Nabbit for [your search term]" — for example, "Hey Siri, search Nabbit for WiFi password." Nabbit will open directly to the search results. The more you search, the better Siri gets at suggesting Nabbit.

How do I delete my data?

To delete a single item, swipe left on it in the library and tap Delete. To delete everything at once, go to Settings > Data > Delete all data. Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting it removes it permanently — there is no cloud backup.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your saved items are stored locally on your iPhone and never uploaded to any server. Nabbit does not have user accounts, cloud storage, or analytics. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.
